Sales Development Representative (SDR)

Overview

SDRs are responsible for outbound prospecting and setting meetings for Account Executives. It's a high-activity, entry-level role into tech sales.

Median Earnings

  • Base Salary: $55,000
  • OTE (On Target Earnings): $75,000
  • Top Performers: $100,000+

How to Break In

Start with learning cold outreach, use LinkedIn, apply to SaaS startups, and showcase grit. No degree required, but communication and hustle are key.

Cold Outreach Templates

Email: Hi [Name], I admire your work at [Company]. I'm passionate about tech sales and would love to learn from your journey. Could we chat?

Phone Opener: Hey [Name], I’ll be quick — I’m a student aiming to break into SaaS sales. Got 2 mins for a quick question?

Top Companies Hiring SDRs

  • Salesforce
  • HubSpot
  • Gong
  • Outreach
  • Oracle

Internships & Programs

Look into LinkedIn’s BDR internship, Oracle Sales Academy, and HubSpot’s Early Career track. Networking on LinkedIn is key.
